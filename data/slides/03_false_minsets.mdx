# Tree isometries
<br />

Let $T$ be a tree with metric $d_T$ and $\phi:T\to T$ its isometry.

<Theorem title="translation length">
$$\tau(\phi) := \inf_{x\in T}d_T(x,\phi(x))\in [0,\infty)$$
</Theorem>

<Theorem title="minset">
$$Min(\phi) := \{x\in T\;:\; d_T(x,\phi(x))=\tau(\phi)\}$$
</Theorem>

We say that $\phi$ is 
- an **eliptic** isometry if $\tau(\phi)=0$
- and a **hyperbolic** isometry otherwise.
