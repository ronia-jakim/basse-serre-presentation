# Fundamental domain - fancier example

### Example 

$PSL(2,\mathbb{Z})$ acts on the hyperbolic plane, identified with the upper half plane $H=\{z\in\mathbb{C}\;:\;Re(z)\geq0\}$. 

The fundamental domain of this action is $F=\{z\in H\;:\;|z|>1,\;|Re(z)|<\frac{1}{2}\}$

<br />

Why? Take any point $z\in H$. WLOG $Im(z)>1$ - otherwise "inversions" and translations will help.

Once we are in $Im(z)>1$ simply apply $\begin{bmatrix}1 & \pm 1 \\ 0 & 1\end{bmatrix}$ untill in $F$.
<br />

**Tree perspective**

y=\{e^{i\theta}\;:\;\frac{\pi}{3}\leq \theta\leq \frac{\theta}{2}\}

- $y$ is an edge with origin point $P=e^{i\pi/3}$ and terminus $Q=i$
